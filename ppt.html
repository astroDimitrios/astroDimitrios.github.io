Teaching Resources

These are some Astronomy powerpoints and other resources that I have used to teach Astronomy and in particular a course on Astronomy and Space Science. The most up to date versions along with pdf copies (and ones with notes) are available here on my Google Drive. Click on the images to be taken to the relevant Google Drive folder.


Useful Dates:

  • Feb 11th - March 8th - Women and Girls in Astronomy start is International Day of Women and Girls in Science, end is International Women's Day
  • March 11th - 20th - British Science Week
  • March 14th - Mars Day (part of Mars week and Mars Hour at 11am), 2022 only atm
  • April 22nd - Earth Day
  • April 22nd - 30th - International Dark Sky Week
  • May 1st - National Space Day (UK)
  • May 7th - International Space Day
  • May 13th - International Astronomy and Astrophysics Competition (IAAC) round 1 deadline
  • July - LaSciL Summer School, linking large observational instruments and their data to school settings
  • September - AstroPi Mission Space Lab applications open
  • Oct 1st - CanSat deadline to register
  • Oct 1st - 10th - World Space Week
  • Oct 1st - 4th - 100 Hours of Astronomy (IAU) ran 2021, unsure 2022


The Royal Astronomical Society has resource links here and the curated International Astronomical Union resources are here. openstax has an open source Astronomy textbook. Also be sure to check out the Global Hands-On Universe project GHOU and the Galileo Teacher Training Program GTTP.

John Kolena maintains a list of image processing resources here. Christine Hirst Bernhardt's Astronomy resource collection. Free book - Exploring the Planets. NISE Celestial Events Calendar - nisenet.org/seasons.
